October 27, 2007

Backwater Burial introduced the new frontman, Metal Mike, at Rocktoberfest 2007 in London, KY. We all had a good time and made some friends with other bands in the area. We sincerely thank Wes for making ths happen and we appreciate everyone who made an effort to come out and rock with us on this chilly October day.



The set list for this show was:

  1. October Wind
  2. Open Your Eyes
  3. The Trooper (Iron Maiden cover)
  4. Mass Graves of the Blind Obediant
  5. Under a Blood Moon
  6. New Religion
